♿ Accessibility Statement 🌟

📅 Effective Date: January 1, 2025

🌟 1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Torres Granite, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience for all users. 🌐

✅ 2. Accessibility Standards

Our website is designed to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines ensure our content is accessible to individuals with various disabilities, including visual, auditory, and cognitive impairments. 🛠️

🔧 3. Features of Our Accessible Website

  • 📖 Text alternatives for non-text content.
  • ⌨️ Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements.
  • 🖥️ Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and assistive technologies.
  • ⚙️ Contrast ratios to ensure text and visuals are readable.

🔄 4. Ongoing Improvements

We are continuously improving the accessibility of our website. User feedback is a vital part of this process, and we welcome suggestions on how we can do better. 💡
